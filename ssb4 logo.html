About SSB

Understanding the Service Selection Board process is the first step towards your success in the Indian Armed Forces

What is SSB?

Service Selection Board is a comprehensive evaluation process to assess Officer-Like Qualities in candidates aspiring to join the Indian Armed Forces.

Why it Matters

SSB is the gateway to prestigious careers in Army, Navy, and Air Force. It's not just a test, but a transformation journey.

5-Day Process

A comprehensive 5-day evaluation covering psychological tests, group tasks, interviews, and conference rounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

The age limit varies by entry: NDA (16.5-19.5 years), CDS (20-25 years for IMA/INA, 20-27 for AFA), and other technical entries have specific age criteria.
There's no limit on SSB attempts. You can appear as many times as you want, provided you meet the age criteria for each entry.
OLQs include effective intelligence, reasoning ability, organizing ability, power of expression, social adaptability, cooperation, sense of responsibility, initiative, self-confidence, speed of decision, ability to influence the group, liveliness, determination, courage, stamina, and physical fitness.
