What is the SSB?

The Services Selection Board (SSB) is a rigorous 5-day evaluation process designed to assess the character, intelligence, and leadership potential of candidates for the Indian Armed Forces.

Screening Test

Day 1 assessment including OIR (Officer Intelligence Rating) tests and PPDT (Picture Perception & Description Test).

Psychological Tests

A comprehensive battery of written tests including TAT, WAT, SRT, and Self-Description to reveal your personality.

GTO & Interview

Group tasks, planning exercises, and a personal interview to evaluate your performance in a team and under pressure.
